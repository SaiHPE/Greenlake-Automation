# Getting help

If you require assistance with HPE Sustainability Insight Center API, you can contact HPE GreenLake support.

1. Open the [HPE GreenLake Platform Support form](https://h41390.www4.hpe.com/support/index.html?form=glsupport).
2. Enter your first name, last name, the company name, and select your preferred contact method.
3. In **Support Issue Details**, select **Dashboard(s) and Monitoring** from the **What do you need help with?** drop-down.
4. Select **Sustainability Dashboard**.
5. Enter additional details on your issue. Providing further detail helps HPE GreenLake support investigate and resolve your issue efficiently.
6. (Optional) Upload a document or file that gives extra context to your issue.
7. Click **Submit**. A ticket is raised with HPE GreenLake support.
